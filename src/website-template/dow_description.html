Understanding which days of the summer are days of peak tourism activity is crucial to prepare the city for large crowds. However, this information alone is not sufficient. We have also analyzed the busiest days of the week and times of the day, allowing us to see more precisely which museums are full and which are underutilized. Figure 15 suggests that the Monday closure of the Uffizi and the Accademia have a considerable impact on the number of entries to other museums in the city. Wednesday and Thursday are the busiest days in terms of museum entries, which could in part be 24 due to there being more users with active cards on Tuesdays, Wednesdays and Thursdays than the rest of the days of the week.
