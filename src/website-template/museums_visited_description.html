Number of museums visited with the Firenzecard between June 1 2016 and September 30 2016.

The most frequent pattern of Firenzecard holders is to visit 6 or 7 attractions. Note that this counts cards , not people entering museums; multiple minors may enter on the same card. Counting the number of people, however, results in a similar distribution. This lets us know that most people visit at least two or three places other than the top four attractions.
