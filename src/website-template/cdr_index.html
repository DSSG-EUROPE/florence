The total estimated presences per day for all types of people in the city of Florence during the summer. We can see that on an average day, one third of the people in the city are tourists. That ratio increases in the month of August as foreign tourism remains somewhat constant, whereas the resident population makes a dip. We can also see cyclic activity that is likely due in part to reduced calling on weekends.
